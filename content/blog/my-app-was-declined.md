Yesterday I applied to add my vibecoded app to izzydroid repo and it got declined. I know vibecoded apps are bad. But let me make the case for my app. 
Have you heard of wallabag? It is a read later open source service that is self hostable. This is very unpopular app I think because their website ui looks so outdated. The project is not abandoned. I decided to use this read later service for my personal use. It has all the features anyone could ask for and it works fine. But here is the thing, the android app is very outdated as well. It does not even have all the features that you get in the web version. For example highlight and notes does not work in their official android app. Which is gonna suck if I am going to use it as my primary read later service. I own two android devices one smartphone and one tablet. So I am not going to be able to use wallabag if the app does not have the basic features. 

So I decided to change it myself. I vibe coded an app that links to your wallabag account and syncs your articles. It has first class support for highlight syncing, tags, among other things. The app follows material ui design and looks good on big screen as well. 

I get it why they do not allow vibecoded apps. They are usually low quality and something that you have made in one sitting. But please I would like to have some exclusions. 
If that is not possible and they do not add it to their repo, I want another repo that allows vibecoded apps as well. I do not mind an app that is written by ai, as long as it fulfills it purpose. 
Thanks for listening to my rant.
