Yesterday I decided to make the curry of chola batura. I bought 250gms of kabli chola and let them soak overnight. I tried to find some recipes for chola batura but I could not find it that might work with the ingrediencts I have. I do not want to follow all the steps for chola batura to make the perfect one as it would take so much time. I am so bored of eating chicken and fish most of the time that I need to try something new. Enough of chicken recipes (I tried 2 recipes, resturant style, so far), I want to make something different. 

Today I went to buy 250g of chicken breasts (so its a chicken recipe now) t use as seasoning in the curry. They did not give me exactly the breast there was some chicken wing also. I cut them into smaller pieces and let it marinate with salt only. I do not have lemons, its not the season I think. 

I prepared the masala with same ingreditents that I use for making chicken curry. 

- 3 big sized onion
- ginger paste one tablespoon
- garlic paste half tablespoon
- turmeric
- coriender powder
- Kashmiri Mirch
