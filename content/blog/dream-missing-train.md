There is this dream I always get. I wait for a train in a wrong platform. When the train enters I run as fast to go to the correct platform, but I have to go through a over bridge. Halfway through the run, I remember I have forgot something. This time it was my bag. I ran back fast and somehow picked up my bag, it wasn’t zipped, then ran back again. But I can’t see the train only announcements. I at first mistakenly go to wrong place and then came back and went to the right platform. By then train has started and I can see the last compartment leaving the platform. I want to call mom, but in a different scene she has been sleeping for the whole day. I asked her to wake up, she did but said my head hurts and slept again. I feel lonely In the platform after missing train I couldn’t reach her. It was evening. I thought I have to go by bus now.

Clearification: The missing train part is what's recurring. I dreamt of this station and scene at least 3-4 times.
