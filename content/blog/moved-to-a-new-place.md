I have moved to a new place from the old one. This time I stayed at one place for less than two months. I now live 1km away from where I used to live before. This is the second time I am changing the place as where I live. First time I was kinda forced to move out, because of little argument with the mess owner and the people living there and this second time is on my own wish.  I have this room for myself and only one more person is going to stay here. 

One of my college friend is moving in with me. He wanted to live in Midnapore and I was like why not take a room together and be roommates. 
Initially I thought that can can move in to to the same building I was on that time. He came checked and then we finalised the place where he is going to stay. Unfortunately after 10-15 days the owner said that the already present guys said that they are going to take the room completely so no place for a new person. That caught me off guard. I started looking for new room to change and after 2-3 days of searching finally found one that almost suits me. 

This room is little bit far from the place where my brother lives, but it is not too far. It is easily possible to walk that distance once every day. So overall with some compromises I like it here. The room is big for 3 persons but I would remove the extra bed ( or keep it standing inside the room). That way the room would be spacious and comfortable. I would need to clean the room as well, it is very dusty and dirty. There was some stuff of the previous person who rented it, I am planning to keep the useful ones and throw out the things I do not need. The washrooms were not in great shape they were all dirty. I cleaned them and they are usable now. For washing dishes we have to go the roof because there is huge space and multiple taps with place to throw out the discarded food items. The place to take out trash is little far but its doable.
