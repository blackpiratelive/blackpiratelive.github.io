Few days ago I decided to make the curry of chola batura. I bought 250gms of kabli chola and let them soak overnight. I tried to find some recipes for chola batura but I could not find it that might work with the ingrediencts I have. I do not want to follow all the steps for chola batura to make the perfect one as it would take so much time. I am so bored of eating chicken and fish most of the time that I need to try something new. Enough of chicken recipes (I tried 2 recipes, resturant style, so far), I want to make something different. 


Today I went to buy 250g of chicken breasts (so its a chicken recipe now) t use as seasoning in the curry. They did not give me exactly the breast there was some chicken wing also. I cut them into smaller pieces and let it marinate with salt only. I do not have lemons, its not the season I think. 

I prepared the masala with same ingreditents that I use for making chicken curry. 

### Ingredients
- chole (white chickpea)
- chicken 250g
- 3 big sized onion
- ginger paste one tablespoon
- garlic paste half tablespoon
- turmeric
- coriender powder
- Kashmiri Mirch
- Sahi Garam Masala
- Salt to taste

I first fried the chopped onions in mustard oil. When it was brown I added ginger garlic paste and also fried it until it started to smell really good. Then I added little bit of water and all other masala. I let it boil and was sirring so the masala does not sit below. I added both the chicken and the chole when the masala was cooked and ready and let it boil until the chole got cooked properly. 

The whole dish did not go well, the taste was meh and I did not get any smell of the chicken. I thought it would make the curry tastier but that did not happen. 

### Mistakes and Learnings
I talked with mom about how it went and she pinpointed few mistakes that I have made. 

- I had to cook the chicken and the chole seperately. 
- It took so long to cook the chole properly, I should have used a pressure cooker. The recipe I saw on youtube also used pressure cooker. 

I am planning to cook it again pretty soon. Overall it wasn't a total disaster.
